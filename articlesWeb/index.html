Holy King James: Life in CTU-Danao Campus
a National Challenger

King Mel James, a student from a humble background, transformed his life through dedication and hard work in programming, specifically in the C language. Growing up in a modest household, Mel's fascination with technology led him to self-study and practice extensively, using online resources and seeking mentorship from teachers and peers.

His determination paid off when he entered a regional coding competition focused on C. After months of preparation, Mel showcased his skills, tackling complex problems with confidence. His efforts culminated in him being crowned the regional champion in the C language category, marking a significant milestone in his journey.

Today, King Mel James continues to inspire others by mentoring young programmers and sharing his knowledge through workshops. His story exemplifies the power of perseverance, proving that success comes from hard work and a passion for learning. Mel remains committed to making a positive impact in the tech community, encouraging others to pursue their dreams.

The ACE

After achieving regional fame as a champion in C programming, King Mel James took the next significant step in his journey by enrolling at Cebu Technological University (CTU) in Danao, where he pursued a degree in computer engineering. His passion and dedication quickly set him apart, earning him the title of the “ace” of the department. Mel became a source of inspiration for his fellow students, representing the values of hard work, determination, and community spirit.

With aspirations of elevating both his name and his university’s reputation, Mel entered a prestigious national programming competition. The event attracted the brightest minds from across the Philippines, each eager to showcase their skills and compete for the title. As Mel prepared for the competition, he relied on the knowledge and experience he had gained throughout his academic journey. On the day

of the event, he remained calm under pressure, demonstrating exceptional problem-solving skills and creativity. Although the competition was fierce, Mel’s efforts paid off when he secured a place in the top five, a remarkable feat among many talented competitors from various regions.

Despite narrowly missing the championship title, Mel saw his performance as a valuable experience rather than a setback. The support from his university and community only fueled his determination to improve further. Inspired by his journey, he vowed to return stronger and more prepared for future competitions. His commitment to continuous growth and his unwavering spirit demonstrated that true champions are defined not just by victories but by their ability to rise, learn, and strive for greatness. With his eyes set on future challenges, King Mel James remains determined to claim the championship for CTU Danao, proving that his story is just beginning.
